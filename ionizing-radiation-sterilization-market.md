<p><strong>The Ionizing Radiation Sterilization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gamma Irradiation</li><li>X-Ray Irradiation</li><li>Electron Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ionizing radiation sterilization market comprises three primary types: gamma irradiation, X-ray irradiation, and electron irradiation. Gamma irradiation utilizes gamma rays from radioactive sources to sterilize medical equipment and products effectively. X-ray irradiation employs high-energy X-rays for deep penetration and uniform sterilization, suitable for sensitive items. Electron irradiation uses high-energy electrons for rapid processing and efficient sterilization of materials, particularly in the pharmaceutical and food industries. Each method offers distinct advantages depending on the application and required dose.</p></p>
