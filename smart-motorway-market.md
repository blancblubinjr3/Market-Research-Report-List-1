<p><p>Smart Motorway market types enhance traffic flow and manage congestion through three main schemes. Controlled Motorways utilize variable speed limits and lane signals to optimize traffic conditions. Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running allows the use of the hard shoulder as a running lane during peak times, improving capacity. All Lane Running schemes eliminate the hard shoulder entirely, enabling all lanes to be utilized for regular traffic, thus maximizing road efficiency and reducing delays. Each type aims to enhance safety and reduce congestion.</p></p>
