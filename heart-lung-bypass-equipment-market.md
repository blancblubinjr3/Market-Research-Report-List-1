<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment</li><li>Double Roller Pump Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart-lung bypass equipment market is primarily segmented into single roller pump and double roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment. Single roller pumps use one roller mechanism to move blood, typically simplifying operation and maintenance. In contrast, double roller pumps feature two rollers, offering enhanced flow rates and better control, making them suitable for more complex surgeries. Both types are essential in cardiac surgery, allowing for temporary artificial circulation to maintain oxygenation and perfusion during procedures.</p></p>
